WHAT WE ARE

Our Vision

We create UGC, shaped by a decade of filmmaking, photography, and music. For us, content has never been just content. It’s vision. The way light shapes a place. The emotion in every frame. The rhythm of movement, in people, cities, and moments. Every shot tells a story. Every color, shadow, and cut is a decision, a feeling.

Today, we bring that perspective into everything we create, from cinematic films to UGC for travel, fashion, and modern brands. Content that feels real, moves fast, and belongs in today’s attention-driven world. We don’t choose between authenticity and quality, we combine both. Now, we document life as it unfolds. Pushing creative boundaries. Capturing experiences as they are, not staged, but felt. Not to follow a formula, but to inspire. To show what’s possible when you fully commit to your vision. Because after years behind the camera, it’s time to tell our own story.

WHO WE ARE

Founder
Ahmed Gad – Cinematographer | Creator | Storyteller

Born in 1997, I've spent over a decade immersing into cinematography, photography, and storytelling—pushing creative boundaries and capturing moments that resonate. My journey began from the ground up, evolving from pure passion into collaborations with global brands like Coca-Cola, Adidas, BMW, Rolex, Red Bull, and Samsung, just to name a few. But for me, it was never just about big names—it was about creating work that moves people.

Every frame tells a story, every shot carries emotion, and every project is a new adventure. I create for the experience, the feeling, to express, inspire, for the art itself and the pursuit of creating something timeless, something that lasts, something real. Because life moves too fast to not capture its chaos, its beauty, and its truth.

When I'm not filming, I'm chasing speed on my sport motorcycle or tackling on the american football field. Because just like in art, in life, you have to take risks to feel alive.
Melissa Ghaly - Creative Director | Creator | Storyteller | DJ

I was born in the Philippines in 1994 and have been defined by an unyielding creative spirit from an early age. In 2009, I ventured into the fashion world, immersing myself in the art of design and product creation—both in theory and practice. After a decade of honing my skills in fashion and visual merchandising, my passion evolved, leading me into professional photography, film, and media design.

Determined to refine my craft, I attended the LIK Academy for Photo & Design, further developing my skills as a digital artist and visual storyteller. But for me, it has never been just about technical mastery—it’s about capturing emotions, telling stories that resonate, and pushing the boundaries of creativity.

Every project is an opportunity to create something meaningful, something real. Whether behind the lens or in the editing room, my work is about more than aesthetics; it’s about translating moments into timeless visual narratives that inspire and move people. My artistry extends beyond photography and film—I bring my creative vision to life through music as a DJ, through writing, and through digital content that reflects my deep passion for personal development.

When I’m not immersed in my creative pursuits, I thrive on adventure, embracing life with the same energy I bring to my work. For me, art and life go hand in hand—both require passion, risk, and a relentless pursuit of expression.
